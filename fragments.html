Fragments

Nigam Sedhai

Fragments is a visual reflection on the way memories fade, leaving behind only the feelings, scents, and impressions that truly mattered. Inspired by my childhood moments of studying, snacking on fruit, and being surrounded by books.

This piece blends Realism to capture the objects I remember vividly, with surreal, abstract forms representing the parts time has blurred.

The subdued, earthy tones invite the viewer to sense the room rather than just see it, evoking the quiet comfort and vintage warmth of simpler days, when life felt both ordinary and infinite.

Year 2025
Medium Acrylic on Canvas
Dimensions 25 × 24 inch - Original Sold
Style Surreal Realism
