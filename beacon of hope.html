Beacon of Hope

Beacon of Hope was created during Nepal’s financial crisis, which began in 2023, a time of uncertainty and hardship. This painting serves as a visual reminder that even in darkness, Nepal remains a land of peace.

At its heart stands a glowing lantern, a powerful symbol of resilience and hope, casting warmth across the canvas and inviting viewers to hold onto the light during trying times.

The backdrop features iconic Nepali temples and traditional decorations, elements deeply rooted in the country’s cultural identity. These motifs reflect the simplicity, beauty, and quiet strength of Nepalese heritage, reminding people that in their roots lies enduring power and unity.

Year 2023
Medium Acrylic on canvas
Dimensions 12 × 16 inches
Style Realism
