Hindu Elephant

Inspired by the vibrant style of Art Sherpa, Cinnamon Cooney, Hindu Elephant brings to life the festive spirit of Asia.

This painting features a beautifully decorated elephant, a common and cherished sight during Hindu celebrations.

Adorned with colorful patterns and leaves, these elephants symbolize peace, strength, and triumph, central values in Hindu culture and tradition.

Year 2020
Medium Acrylic on canvas
Dimensions 18 x 24
Style Contemporary
