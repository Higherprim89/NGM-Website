The Artist

Nigam Sedhai

This piece represents a girl, fully immersed in her music, and embodies a sense of joy and self-assurance. With her cute hat tilted just right and headphones wrapped around her ears, she's in sync with her favorite playlist as she strums her guitar.

Her expression and relaxed posture capture the essence of someone who has found deep contentment and fulfillment in her creative outlet.

The painting radiates a serene confidence, showing a person who, free from external pressures, embraces her passion wholeheartedly, soaking in the happiness that her artistic pursuit brings.

Year 2024
Medium Acrylic on canvas
Dimensions 16 × 20 in.
Location Art Attack Gallery
Style Cubism
