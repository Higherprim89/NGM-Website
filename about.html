About the Artist

 The creative journey behind Art Attack by NGM

My Artistic Journey

Self-taught artist Nigam was born and raised in Nepal, where her creative journey began as a deeply personal form of emotional expression. What started as a way to channel inner turmoil during her preteen years has since evolved into a conscious and powerful choice, one that continues to shape her identity and purpose as an artist. Now based in the DFW area, Nigam is an active member of the Visual Arts Guild of Frisco and the Visual Arts League of Allen, frequently showcasing and selling her work in local exhibitions

Nigam crafts compelling visual narratives that explore the complexities of emotion, identity, and transformation. Her work draws heavily from her life experiences, cultural roots, and ongoing artistic journey. Through a bold fusion of geometric lines, surrealism, realism, and cubism, she transforms discomfort into meaning, inviting viewers to engage, reflect, and find catharsis. Each piece is not only an expression of her truth but also an offering of connection and shared human experience.

Her artwork was published in Physician Outlook Magazine in 2020, marking a milestone in her growing recognition as a distinctive voice in the art realm.

Artist Statement

I paint to process discomfort and transform it into something meaningful. My work blends geometric lines, surrealism, realism, and cubism to create visual narratives rooted in my life, culture, and artistic journey.
Each piece is a release, an invitation for others to find connection and catharsis.
